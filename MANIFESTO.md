## The Structured Incompleteness Model

Alexandria follows the Mendeleev Principle.

Like the periodic table,
the lattice is defined before all positions are filled.

Events populate coordinates.
They do not define the grid.

The grid exists first.

---

The system behaves like a constrained physical field:
- Intents are perturbations.
- Invariants are conservation laws.
- Domains are basis vectors.
- Replay is relaxation into canonical equilibrium.

Alexandria does not search for futures.
It canonizes the past.

The power of the system is not in what it contains,
but in what it refuses to become.
